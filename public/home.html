Welcome to Snoot Land!

Story

The Merriam Webster Dictionary defines the word "snoot" as:

  1. snout, nose
  2. a grimace expressive of contempt
  3. a snooty person: snob
  4. (verb) to treat with disdain: to look down one's nose at

In modern lexicography (the practice of compiling dictionaries), however, the term refers to a person who adheres rigidly to tradition and authority when it comes to language and the usage of words. These snoots, also known as prescriptivists, are at war with the descriptivists, the liberal linguists who assert that authority and tradition only serve to stifle creativity. It's a battle too convoluted to delineate here, but the point is that you are about to embark on a quest to Snoot Castle to defeat King Snoot in the only way possible: by out-snooting him. Snoots derive their life force from pointing out and correcting others' mistakes. Deprive them of that by answering their questions correctly, and you'll accede to King Snoot's throne!

Instructions

Use the following commands:

  • /startGame to start (or restart) the game
  • Use the links to move and submit answers.
  • /viewInventory to view your inventory
  • /viewHealth to see your stats
  • /describeRoom to describe the current room
  • /save to save the game. Make sure to copy the id so that you can load it later. Note that the game can't be saved while a fight is in progress.
  • /load?id=insert+id+here to load from a saved game
